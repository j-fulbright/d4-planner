## üí° Paladin-Specific Pro Tips

### 1. **Glyph Socket Strategy**
- The center glyph socket affects nodes within its radius
- For Paladins: Use **Fervor** or **Judgment** glyphs
- Fervor: Boosts Critical Strike for Fervent legendary
- Judgment: Enhances Sentencing legendary power

### 2. **Legendary Board Priority**
Based on your build:
- **Zealot Builds:** Preacher (Attack Speed) ‚Üí Fervent (Crit)
- **Arbiter Builds:** Divinity (Vulnerable) ‚Üí Sentencing (Judging)
- **Tank Builds:** Castle (Armor) ‚Üí Shield Bearer (Block) ‚Üí Endure (Fortify)
- **Party Support:** Beacon (Auras)

### 3. **Strength Scaling**
- Paladin scales well with Strength
- Every +10 Strength ‚âà +1% damage
- Rare nodes are still 20-40x better value per point

### 4. **Synergy with Legendaries**
- **Castle:** +2% Armor from Tenacity = +3.6% damage
- **Shield Bearer:** Block builds benefit from defensive rare nodes
- **Fervent:** Crit builds want Raw Power/Iron Strength damage
- **Preacher:** Attack Speed scales with damage bonuses

### 5. **Arbiter vs Zealot**
- **Arbiter Form:** Prioritize elite damage (Iron Strength)
- **Zealot Skills:** Prioritize raw damage (Raw Power)
- **Hybrid:** Take both for +50% combined damage
