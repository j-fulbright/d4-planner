## ğŸ¯ Your 36-Point Strategy

### **Goal 1: Reach the GATE (Top of Board)**
**Points needed:** ~15 nodes

**How:**
- Start at the starting node (bottom center)
- Build a connected path going TOWARD the top
- Each node you allocate must TOUCH a node you already allocated
- Follow the CENTER of the board (stay in the middle, don't go to far edges)
- You'll see the path naturally form going upward
- Stop when you reach the GATE node at the top

**What you'll get:**
- Gate unlocked (can attach legendary boards)
- ~75 total primary stats (mixed Str/Dex/Will/Int)

---

### **Goal 2: Allocate BRAWN Rare Node**
**Points needed:** ~6 additional nodes (21 total so far)

**How to find BRAWN:**
- **Visual:** GOLD/ORANGE node (very obvious, different from brown common nodes)
- **Name:** Hover over it - tooltip will say "Brawn"
- **Stats:** Tooltip shows `+20.0% Damage` and `+4.0% Maximum Life`
- **Location:** In the lower area of the board, should be visible from your starting path

**How to allocate:**
- From your allocated path (the 15 nodes to gate), find which node is CLOSEST to Brawn
- Build a connected path from that node to Brawn
- Each node must touch the previous one
- Allocate Brawn rare node when you reach it

**What you'll get:**
- +20% Damage bonus
- +4% Maximum Life

---

### **Goal 3: Allocate TENACITY Rare Node**
**Points needed:** ~6 additional nodes (27 total so far)

**How to find TENACITY:**
- **Visual:** GOLD/ORANGE node (another rare)
- **Name:** Hover over it - tooltip will say "Tenacity"
- **Stats:** Tooltip shows `+4.0% Maximum Life` and `+2.0% Total Armor`
- **Location:** In the lower area of the board, near Brawn

**How to allocate:**
- From your allocated nodes (including Brawn), find which is CLOSEST to Tenacity
- Build a connected path to Tenacity
- Each node must touch the previous one
- Allocate Tenacity rare node when you reach it

**What you'll get:**
- +4% Maximum Life
- +2% Total Armor

---

### **Goal 4: Fill to 36 Points**
**Points needed:** 9 more nodes (36 total)

**What to allocate:**
1. **Nodes around the GLYPH SOCKET** (red circle in center of board)
   - Glyphs boost nodes in their radius
   - Allocating near the socket = better bonuses

2. **Efficient connectors**
   - Connect your rare nodes to your main path
   - Fill any gaps in your allocation

3. **Any remaining touching nodes**
   - Just make sure they touch your allocated nodes

**What you'll get:**
- ~45 additional stats (mixed)
- Better glyph socket coverage
